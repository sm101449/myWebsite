All about The last of Us

The Last of Us (TLOU) is a post-apocalyptic action-adventure game set in a world devastated by a fungal infection that turns humans into aggressive creatures. Key Points: Main Characters: Joel, a hardened survivor, and Ellie, a teenage girl who is immune to the infection. Plot: Joel must escort Ellie across the U.S. to a group that hopes to use her immunity to create a vaccine. Themes: Survival, the bond between Joel and Ellie, and moral choices in dire situations. WHO IS ELLIE? Ellie is a central character in the video game series "The Last of Us" developed by Naughty Dog. Set in a post-apocalyptic world ravaged by a fungal infection that turns people into deadly creatures, Ellie is a teenage girl who has grown up in this harsh environment.
