WHO IS ELLIE?

Ellie, also known as Ellie Williams, is the main protagonist of "The Last of Us" (TLOU) series. A survivor of a fungal infection that nearly wiped out humanity, Ellie is known for her strength, resilience, and emotional depth. She is also an exceptional marksman, capable of handling firearms and stealth techniques to survive in the dangerous world of TLOU.

She is an important character in both the video game and its television adaptation. She forms a close bond with Joel, another main character in the series, as they journey across a post-apocalyptic America.

WHO IS JOEL?

Joel is a central character in "The Last of Us" (TLOU), a popular video game series and its TV adaptation. He is a hardened survivor who lost his daughter at the start of the outbreak and is emotionally scarred as a result. Joel plays a key role in guiding Ellie across the post-apocalyptic United States, with both characters developing a deep bond during their journey.
